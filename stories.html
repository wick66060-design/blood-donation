Real Stories, Real Heroes

Hear from those whose lives were transformed by a single donation.

I never realized the importance of giving blood until my sister was in a severe car accident on the Ethio-China road. She survived purely because strangers had donated blood. I have become a regular donor ever since.

Dawit Y.

Voluntary Donor - Addis Ababa

During complications with the birth of my first child, I lost an alarming amount of blood. Without the two units of blood ready at the hospital, neither of us would be here today. Every donor is an angel.

Senait T.

Blood Recipient - Hawassa

I used to be terrified of needles! But when our university organized a blood drive, my friends encouraged me. It was literally a ten-minute pinch. Now, knowing I am helping 3 people each time is the best feeling in the world.

Abel M.

University Student Group
