Why Donate Blood?

It's a small act that makes a massive impact. Here is why you should consider donating today.

The Unseen Benefits

  • Free Health Checkup: Before donating, you get a mini physical checkup including pulse, blood pressure, body temperature, and hemoglobin levels.
  • Cardiovascular Health: Regular blood donation helps lower iron levels in the blood, which may reduce the risk of heart disease.
  • Mental Wellbeing: Knowing you have actively saved lives boosts your mental health and gives a sense of community responsibility.
