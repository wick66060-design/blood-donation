Debunking the Myths

Misinformation stops thousands from saving lives. Let us separate fact from fiction.

Fact: Your body replaces the lost fluids within 24 hours, and red blood cells within weeks. You can resume normal activities the very next day!

Fact: You will only feel a quick mild pinch. Most donors say it's completely painless and over in less than 10 minutes.

Fact: Donating is 100% safe. Sterile, single-use, disposable equipment is used. You cannot contract diseases by donating.

Fact: As long as you maintain a balanced diet rich in plant-based iron (beans, spinach, lentils), your iron levels will likely be fine.
