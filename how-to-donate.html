How to Donate Blood

The process is simple, safe, and takes very little time.

1 Check Eligibility

Age 18-65, weight >50kg, and generally in good health. Must not have donated in the last 3 months (men) or 4 months (women). Click to see more.

Drag to rotate

2 Register

Fill out a short questionnaire regarding your health history and travel to ensure safety for both you and the recipient. Click to reveal.

Drag to rotate

3 Screening

A quick health check including pulse, blood pressure, temperature, and a tiny finger prick for iron levels. Click for details.

Drag to rotate

4 Donate

The actual donation takes about 8-10 minutes. You can relax, listen to music, or talk with the staff. Click to see the process.

Drag to rotate

5 Recover

Rest for 10-15 minutes while enjoying complimentary juice and biscuits before leaving the center. Click for post-care.

Where to Donate?

Visit the National Blood Bank Service headquarters near Stadium, or check for our mobile blood donation trucks operating daily in major plazas around Addis Ababa.
