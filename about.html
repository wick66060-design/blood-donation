About Blood Donation

Understanding the life-saving impact of voluntary blood donation in Ethiopia and across the continent.

Accidents & Emergencies

Traffic accidents and unexpected emergencies account for a massive demand for blood transfusions daily in Addis Ababa hospitals.

Maternal Care

Severe bleeding during delivery and after childbirth is a major cause of mortality, illnesses, and long-term disability. Blood saves mothers.

Chronic Illnesses

Patients suffering from conditions like anemia, cancer, and varying blood disorders require regular transfusions to survive.

The Ethiopian Context

While the WHO recommends that all blood donations should be 100% voluntary and unpaid, many regions heavily rely on family replacement donors. The Ethiopian National Blood Bank Services is pushing to change this culture among the urban youth, ensuring safe, adequate blood supplies are always available without placing pressure on patients' families during times of crisis.
